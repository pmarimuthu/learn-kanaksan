# Chapter 13 — Oscillations

<ConceptMeta chapter="13" section="13.1–13.8" page="304–328" />

Oscillations are everywhere — from atoms vibrating in a crystal to the swing of a pendulum and the oscillating current in a circuit.

## Concepts in This Chapter

- [Periodic Motion & SHM](./simple-harmonic-motion) — Definition and characteristics of simple harmonic motion
- [Energy in SHM](./energy-in-shm) — Kinetic and potential energy exchange
- [Simple Pendulum](./simple-pendulum) — Period, amplitude independence, and applications
- [Damped Oscillations](./damped-oscillations) — How friction reduces amplitude over time
- [Resonance](./resonance) — Forced oscillations and the resonance phenomenon

## Why This Matters

Oscillations are the basis of all clocks, musical instruments, radio waves, and molecular vibrations. Understanding resonance is critical for safe engineering design.

# Chapter 14 — Waves

<ConceptMeta chapter="14" section="14.1–14.10" page="334–364" />

Waves transfer energy without transferring matter. Sound, light, water ripples and seismic waves are all wave phenomena governed by the same underlying mathematics.

## Concepts in This Chapter

- [Wave Motion](./wave-motion) — Types of waves, wavelength, frequency, wave speed
- [Speed of a Transverse Wave](./speed-of-waves) — What determines how fast a wave travels
- [Superposition & Standing Waves](./standing-waves) — Interference, nodes, antinodes
- [Beats](./beats) — What happens when two similar frequencies combine
- [Doppler Effect](./doppler-effect) — How motion changes observed frequency

## Why This Matters

Waves underpin music, medical imaging (ultrasound, MRI), earthquakes, communications and optics.
